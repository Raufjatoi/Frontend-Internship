Ed Sheeran


Ed Sheeran, a British singer-songwriter, has achieved global superstardom. He began his career busking on London streets, independently releasing EPs and building an online following. His debut album, "+" (2011), featuring hits like "The A Team," propelled him to fame. Subsequent albums, "x" (2014) and "รท" (2017), produced chart-topping singles like "Thinking Out Loud" and "Shape of You." Sheeran's live performances are known for their intimate and energetic atmosphere. He has embarked on extensive world tours, selling out arenas and stadiums worldwide. His "Divide Tour" ranks among the highest-grossing tours in history. Beyond his solo career, Sheeran has collaborated with numerous artists and used his platform to support various charities. His unique blend of genres, captivating songwriting, and engaging live performances have earned him a devoted global fanbase.
